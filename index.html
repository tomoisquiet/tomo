what is tomo

not a coach. not a therapist.
just a friend who pays attention.

tomo is a warm space where you can check in with yourself, track how you feel, and start noticing the patterns that shape your days.

every day, tomo asks one simple question: how are you doing?

log your mood and energy in seconds. build small habits. write in your journal. over time, tomo starts connecting the dots -- noticing that your mood dips on certain days, that exercise changes how you feel, that your energy follows patterns you never saw before.

what makes tomo different

most wellness apps feel clinical.
tomo feels like a friend.

🌱

he's alive

tomo blinks, wiggles, watches clouds with you. he's not a logo -- he's a companion who's always there.

🌙

he knows what time it is

the app changes with the hour. morning greetings, evening check-ins, late-night gentleness. it meets you where you are.

💜

he never guilts you

no streak anxiety. no red notifications. no "you missed a day!" on tough days, tomo softens. on good days, he celebrates quietly.
